    • About Our VARIT
    Symposium :

    VARIT stands for a combination of various IT-related events. The name was coined in 2011 by students of our IT department. The first VARIT event was conducted in 2011 and has since been held annually, organized by final-year students with support from first-year and second-year students. How many of us know? Our college does not charge any registration fee from participants, and we provide food and hospitality free of cost. VARIT is a proud legacy of Jamal Mohamed College, organized by the CS, IT, and CA departments.
